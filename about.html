Rabika Help & Brand Guide

About Us

The sections are arranged so customers can quickly understand Rabika, shopping, fit, fabric, and support.

Shipping & Delivery

+ How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered within 2–4 business days across India. Local cities like Haridwar, Dehradun, Rishikesh, and Roorkee typically receive orders in 2–4 days. Remote areas may take up to 7 days.

+ Do you deliver all over India?

Yes, we deliver to all serviceable pin codes across India including tier 2 and tier 3 cities. You can check delivery availability by entering your pin code on the product page before placing your order.

Orders & Tracking

+ How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an SMS and email with your tracking link. You can also track your order by visiting our Track Order page and entering your order number.

+ Can I cancel or modify my order?

Orders can be cancelled or modified within 2 hours of placement. After that, the order moves into processing and cannot be changed. Please contact us immediately at MoreInfo@Rabika.in or WhatsApp us on +91 70604 92443 if you need to make changes.

+ I haven't received my order yet. What should I do?

If your order hasn't arrived within 7 business days of placing it, please reach out to us with your order number at moreinfo@rabika.in. We will investigate with our courier partner and resolve it within 48 hours.

Sizing & Fit

+ How do I find my size?

We carry sizes XS (32), S (34), M (36), and L (38). These refer to your bust measurement in inches. Visit our Size Guide for a full measurement chart. When in doubt, we recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit.

+ Do Rabika products run true to size?

Yes — our kurtis are designed to run true to size with a relaxed fit. If you prefer a more fitted look, you can size down. The product page for each kurti mentions the exact fit and the size our model is wearing.

Payments & COD

+ Is Cash on Delivery available?

Yes — COD is available across most pin codes in India. You can select Cash on Delivery at checkout. Please note that COD orders require a small confirmation before dispatch to reduce failed deliveries.

+ What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods including UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), debit and credit cards, net banking, and Cash on Delivery. All online payments are processed securely.

Fabric & Care

+ What fabrics do Rabika products use?

Most of our products are made from 100% pure cotton — soft, breathable, and perfect for India's climate. Some styles use a cotton-linen blend for added structure. The exact fabric is mentioned on each product page under the Fabric & Care section. e.

+ How should I wash Rabika products?

We recommend gentle machine wash in cold water or hand wash. Always dry in shade — avoid direct sunlight to prevent colour fading. Do not bleach. Iron on medium heat inside out. Full care instructions are printed on the garment label.

+ Will the colour fade after washing?

Our fabrics are pre-washed and colour-treated to minimise fading. Following the care instructions — cold wash, shade dry, no bleach — will keep the colours vibrant for a long time. Some natural colour variation in the first wash is normal for pure cotton fabrics.
