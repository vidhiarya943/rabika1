Refund & Exchange Policy

At Rabika, we value your satisfaction and want you to feel confident in your purchase. This Refund & Exchange Policy explains the conditions under which refunds or exchanges are accepted.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are available for products that are defective, damaged, or incorrect. To qualify, you must notify us within 7 days of receiving your order. The product must be unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by the original receipt.

2. Eligibility for Exchanges

Exchanges are available for products that are defective, damaged, or incorrect. To qualify, you must notify us within 7 days of receiving your order. The product must be unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by the original receipt.

3. Non-Refundable Items

Certain items such as discounted products, final sale items, and personalized/custom orders are not eligible for refunds or exchanges.

4. Process for Refunds & Exchanges

To initiate a refund or exchange, please contact our support team at Contact Page. Provide your order number, reason for return, and supporting photos if applicable.

5. Shipping Costs

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or incorrect. In such cases, Rabika will cover the return shipping charges.

6. Refund Timeline

Approved refunds will be processed within 7–10 business days to your original payment method. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Return and Exchange Policy, please contact us:
